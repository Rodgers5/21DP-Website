WHY 21ST DEAF PROGRAMME?

21DP was founded to focus on the welfare of the deaf individuals within the society, with the aim of lifting them up and creating opportunities for them. It all started with the good spirit of the deaf individual to help other deaf individuals who are unable to get opportunities in the economic and business world and make it a better place for them to live.
